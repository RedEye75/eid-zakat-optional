To be eligible to receive zakat, the recipient must be poor and/or needy.
A poor person is someone whose property,
in excess of his basic requirements, does not reach the nisab threshold.
The recipient must not belong to your immediate family;
your spouse, children, parents and grandparents cannot receive your zakat.
